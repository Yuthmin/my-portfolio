ABOUT ME
University of Moratuwa

Driven by curiosity,
shaped by ambition.

I'm W.D. Yuthmin Dilana, an undergraduate pursuing a degree in Information Technology and Management (ITM) at the University of Moratuwa — Sri Lanka's leading technical university.

My journey began with completing Advanced Level examinations, followed by professional experience at People's Bank, Mahara Branch, where I gained firsthand insight into how institutions operate at scale. That experience deepened my passion for applying technology to solve real business challenges.

Today I study at UOM, exploring the intersection of IT and management — from web development and C programming to microcontroller engineering and organisational principles. My goal is to build ventures where technology drives meaningful business outcomes.
